Account Deletion Request

Dancing Feet | Last Updated: December 2024

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your Dancing Feet account and associated data, please send an email to:

xyzeee.code@gmail.com

Required Information

Please include the following in your email:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Username (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional, helps us improve)

What Happens Next

1 We will send a confirmation email to verify your identity
2 Once verified, your account and data will be deleted within 30 days
3 You will receive a final confirmation when deletion is complete

Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Event history and participation records
  • Any content you have posted

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal or security purposes:

  • Transaction records (required for financial/legal compliance)
  • Anonymized usage analytics (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations

Retained data will be kept only as long as legally required.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or your data, please contact us at xyzeee.code@gmail.com
