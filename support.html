How We Can Help

Our support team can assist you with:

  • Technical Issues: App crashes, bugs, or performance problems
  • Account Help: Login issues, password reset, or account recovery
  • Feature Questions: How to use specific features in the app
  • Feedback: Suggestions for improvements or new features
  • Privacy Concerns: Questions about your data and privacy

What to Include in Your Email

To help us assist you faster, please include:

  • A clear description of your issue or question
  • Your device type and operating system version
  • App version (found in Settings)
  • Screenshots if applicable

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 24-48 hours. During busy periods, it may take a bit longer, but we will get back to you as soon as we can.
