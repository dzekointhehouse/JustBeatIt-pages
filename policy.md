## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password (stored securely as a hash).
- **Profile Information:** Name, profile picture, and any other information you choose to add to your profile.
- **User Content:** Text, images, videos, and other content you upload, including practice sessions, dance content, and notes.
- **Event Information:** Details about events you create, including event name, description, date, time, location, and recurrence settings.
- **Communications:** Messages or feedback you send to us.

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data:** Features you use, actions you take within the App, and timestamps of your activity.
- **Location Data:** With your permission, we collect your geographic location to show nearby events and provide location-based features. You can disable location access through your device settings.
- **Push Notification Tokens:** If you enable notifications, we collect tokens necessary to send you event reminders and updates.

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- **App Store Providers:** When you make a purchase, Apple App Store or Google Play Store processes your payment. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not have access to your full payment details.
- **Authentication Providers:** If you sign in using a third-party service (e.g., Google, Apple), we receive basic profile information as permitted by your privacy settings with that service.
